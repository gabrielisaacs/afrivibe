We are a team of Software Engineers brought together by a shared goal to bring a fashion house to life.

"

AfriVibe is the result of our collective effort, creativity, and collaboration. As part of this project, we worked together to design and develop a platform that reflects the identity of a modern African fashion brand. This project is not just an assignment, but a representation of our ability to turn ideas into practical solutions.

Within the team, members contributed in different ways based on their strengths, from design and user experience to development and problem-solving. Those with technical experience supported others by putting ideas into practice while also guiding and sharing knowledge throughout the process. This allowed everyone to learn, grow, and actively participate.

Every decision we made, from the brand name to the design direction and implementation, was carried out collectively through majority votes, ensuring that everyone had a voice and ownership of the final outcome.
